Welcome to OmniCalc. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about data practices associated with our application. Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
IP address
General location data (based on IP address)
Device information (model, operating system, screen size)
App usage and interaction data
Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)


Google may use this information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences and settings. For more details on how Google handles your data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Information from Sites or Apps That Use Its Services.

You can manage your ad personalization preferences through your device settings or by visiting Google's Ad Settings.
